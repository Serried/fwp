Chicken Breast Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, chopped
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk (or almond milk)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Nutrition Facts:

  • Calories: 350 kcal
  • Protein: 40 g
  • Carbohydrates: 30 g
  • Fat: 5 g
  • Fiber: 3 g

Availability:

  • Global
